Wavin’ Flag (国风 mix) - MeloAlright

点击即可播放🤔

原旋律改自飘扬的旗帜 Wavin’ Flag 中文歌词版本名为旗开得胜
“Wavin’ Flag” is a song by Somali-Canadian artist K’naan from his album Troubadour.
